Overview

JD Vault is an enterprise digital asset management product that helps you store, manage and distribute images, videos, rich media files, documents, and other digital media securely and efficiently.


Key features:

  • Digital assets are organized in a central location, automating workflows and streamlining content creation
  • Seamless integration and organization of all assets
  • Sophisticated search capabilities  
  • Customized options for metadata
  • Apply an action to multiple assets
  • Track all asset activity down to the user
  • Generate reports based on various criteria
  • Role based access with permission levels for all users

Benefits:

  • A simple easy to use system, simple portal to upload, store, search, find, access and share digital assets from anywhere and anytime
  • Single Source of All Media: It has a centralized location to store, catalog, index, search and find pictures, videos, and other collateral, and make them available to all the  users. The Digital Asset Management helps teams find the most up-to-date digital assets and collaborate more efficiently with colleagues.
  • Improved Productivity: Increases Productivity as it simplifies everyday processes such as searching for specific images, establishing copyrights and sharing large files, enabling you to save time and hassle when working with media.
  • Collaborate to produce high quality products: JD vault provides the roles, rules, workflow, and collaboration capabilities to help you easily manage the process and ensure your product information is accurate and complete.
  • Easily control the asset information: Gives you complete control over how your assets are represented.
  • Deliver consistent product experience: Eliminate inconsistent and incomplete content and improve the quality and consistency of asset metadata across all channels. Deliver a compelling product experience to drive great customer experiences.
